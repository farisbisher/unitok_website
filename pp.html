UniTok Privacy Policy

Effective: 23-09-2025
Reading time: 8 min
Version: 1.0

This Privacy Policy explains how UniTok ("we", "our", "us") collects, uses, shares, and protects your personal data when you use the UniTok mobile application and related services.

TL;DR Quick Summary ‚ñº
  • We collect your account info, content you create, and usage data to provide our service
  • Camera access is only used when you take photos/videos - never in the background
  • We don't sell your personal information to anyone
  • You can access, update, or delete your data at any time
  • We use industry-standard security measures to protect your information
  • Contact us anytime at otp@unitokapp.com with questions

1 Information We Collect

üîó ‚ñº

We collect the types of information described below to provide, secure, and improve the app.

  • Account Information: email address, username, profile details you provide.
  • User-Generated Content: posts, comments, images, videos, and any media you upload or create within UniTok.
  • Authentication & Security Data: hashed passwords, authentication tokens, device identifiers, login timestamps, and IP addresses to protect accounts and prevent fraud.
  • Usage & Diagnostics: app usage logs, analytics, crash reports, performance data to improve reliability and features.
  • Camera & Media Access: we request access to the device camera and/or photo library only when you explicitly choose to capture or select media inside the app. We do not use the camera in the background.
  • Optional Data: location or contacts only if you explicitly enable related features (and after you grant permission).

2 How We Use Your Information

üîó ‚ñº

Your information helps us deliver and improve our services.

  • Provide core app features (account management, posting, viewing content, comments, likes).
  • Process and store media you capture or upload for display inside UniTok.
  • Detect, prevent, and investigate abuse, spam, fraud, and security incidents.
  • Send important service messages (account, security, and policy updates).
  • Analyze and improve the app (analytics and diagnostics).
  • Comply with legal obligations and respond to lawful requests from authorities.

3 Camera & Media Specifics

üîó ‚ñº

Short summary: Camera access is only used when you intentionally take a photo or record a video inside UniTok. There is no continuous or background recording.

  • Permission: the app requests android.permission.CAMERA (and photo/media storage access when needed). The permission is requested at runtime and you can revoke it at any time via system settings.
  • Usage: media captured is uploaded only if you confirm/submit it in the app. We do not upload camera feed automatically.
  • Retention: uploaded media is stored so long as your account is active or as required for troubleshooting, moderation, or legal reasons.

4 Data Sharing & Disclosure

üîó ‚ñº

We do not sell your personal information. We may share data in the following limited circumstances:

  • Service Providers: trusted vendors that perform hosting, analytics, crash reporting, messaging, and content delivery on our behalf under contractual confidentiality obligations.
  • Legal & Safety: when required by law, to respond to legal process, or to protect rights, property, safety, or to enforce our Terms of Service.
  • Business Transfers: in the event of a merger, sale, or asset transfer, user data may be transferred; we will require the acquiring entity to honor this policy.
  • Aggregated Data: we may share aggregated or de-identified analytics that cannot reasonably identify you.

5 Data Retention

üîó ‚ñº

We retain personal data as long as your account exists and for a reasonable period thereafter to comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, enforce agreements, and for legitimate business purposes such as security and abuse prevention.

If you delete your account, we will remove or anonymize your personal data within a reasonable timeframe, except where we are required to retain data for legal, tax, or safety reasons.

6 Your Rights & Choices

üîó ‚ñº

You have control over certain uses of your information:

  • Access & Correction: you can view and update your profile information from the app.
  • Permissions: you may grant or revoke camera, storage, location, or other permissions through your device settings.
  • Delete Account: see Section 9 below for deletion options and process.
  • Opt-out of Analytics: where applicable, follow in-app settings or contact us to limit analytics tracking.

7 Children

üîó ‚ñº

UniTok is intended for users who meet the minimum age required by local laws. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children who are below the legal minimum age. If you believe a child has provided us personal data without parental consent, please contact us and we will promptly delete the data where required.

üîó ‚ñº

We implement administrative, technical, and physical safeguards designed to protect personal data against unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, and destruction. üîó ‚ñº

  1. In-App: Profile ‚Üí Settings ‚Üí Delete Account (recommended).

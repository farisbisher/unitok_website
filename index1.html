Delete Your UniTok Account

Process time: Up to 30 days
Verification: Email confirmation required

We're sorry to see you go. Please fill out the form below to request the deletion of your UniTok account and all associated data.

TL;DR Quick Summary ▼
  • Fill out the deletion request form below with your registered email
  • We'll send a confirmation email to verify you own the account
  • Click the link in the email to authorize the deletion
  • Our support team will be notified and process your request
  • Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone

2 Important Information

▼

Please read the following information carefully before proceeding with your deletion request:

⚠ Warning

  • Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone
  • All your data, including profile information, history, and preferences will be permanently removed
  • You will lose access to any subscriptions or services linked to this account
  • This process may take up to 30 days to complete

💡 Before Deleting

  • Download any data you want to keep
  • Cancel any active subscriptions
  • Make sure you have access to the email address associated with your account

3 What Data Will Be Deleted

▼

Once your deletion request is verified and confirmed, the following data will be permanently deleted from our systems:

  • Account credentials - Email address and password
  • Profile information - Username, display name, bio, profile picture
  • User preferences - Settings and configurations
  • Activity history - Usage data and interaction logs
  • Associated content - Any content or data you've created
Note: Some anonymized data may be retained for security, fraud prevention, and legal compliance purposes.
